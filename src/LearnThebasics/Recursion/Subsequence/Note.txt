What is a Subsequence..?
--> It is a contigous / non contiguos sequence which follows the order.
--> A subarray is only contiguos but a subsequence can be both
--> And the order of the numbers in subsequence cannot be changed.
--> eg [1,2,3] --> Subsequences are 1, 2, 3, {1,2}, {1,3}, {2,3}, {1,2,3}, {} Other shuffled Combinations
    will not be considered.
    A subarray can be a Sequence.
